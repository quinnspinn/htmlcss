A hairdresser is a person whose occupation is to cut or style hair in order to change or maintain a person's image.
This is achieved using a combination of hair coloring, haircutting, and hair texturing techniques.
Most hairdressers are professionally licensed as either a hairdresser, a barber or a cosmetologist.
